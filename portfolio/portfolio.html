About Me

Hello, My name is Joe Palomino, Palomino spelt just like the horse. I'm a data analyst turned to the light side of web development and design. I hope to one day become a programming master or wizard; both titles seem fitting. I currently live in beautiful Salt Lake City. I'm a husband to an amazing wife and a Father to two beautiful chlidren.

I've created this blog to share my experiences both struggles and succeses in learning how to program. See, its only been recently that I've discovered the beauty in the art of programming; so the goal of this blog is to help those who too have now seen the light but, might not know where to start or what recources are availble. I'll be sharing how I got started, recources I use, in depth case studies, and my thoughts on prograamming principles and trends. I hope to one day create something that will make people's lives just a little better. This is an exciting time to live in and things will only keep getting better; so join me at the frontlines of change. Feel free to follow me and ask me questions on Twitter and Github. Thanks for stopping by.
